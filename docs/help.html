Getting Started

Upload your first manual in seconds
1
Tap the + Upload button in the action bar at the bottom of the screen.
2
Select your POH or AFM PDF from your Files app, iCloud Drive, or any connected storage.
3
Wait a few seconds while the manual loads. It is saved to your device automatically.
4
Your manual opens immediately. Use ◀ ▶ to navigate pages, or tap 📚 to jump to a section.
✈️ Tip: POH Reader works completely offline. Your manuals are stored on your device and never uploaded to any server.
📚

Library

Save and switch between multiple manuals

POH Reader can store multiple PDFs at once — your POH, MEL, supplements, or any other document.

1
Tap the 📚 icon in the action bar to open the Library sheet.
2
Tap + Upload new PDF to add another manual.
3
Tap Open next to any saved manual to switch to it instantly.
4
Tap Delete to remove a manual from the library.
💡 Tip: The currently open manual is marked with ▶. Your last opened manual is automatically restored when you reopen the app.

Search vs Ask

Two different tools for finding information

POH Reader has two ways to find information. They work differently and are best for different situations:

🔍 Search

  • Finds exact text in the PDF
  • Best for numbers and limits
  • Try: "Vref", "105 KIAS", "oil pressure"
  • Shows page and context
  • Free feature

🎧 Ask PRO

  • Understands keywords and topics
  • Best for procedures and emergencies
  • Try: "flap fail", "engine fire", "crosswind"
  • Returns ranked page results with Jump buttons
  • Works 100% offline
✈️ Example: Looking for the maximum crosswind limit? Use Search and type "crosswind" to find the exact number. Dealing with a flap failure procedure? Use Ask and type "flap fail" to jump straight to the emergency page.
⚠️ Always verify all information in the official POH/AFM. POH Reader is a study and reference tool. The pilot in command is solely responsible for aircraft operation.

Read Aloud PRO

Listen to your manual — get the best voice

POH Reader uses Apple's built-in text-to-speech engine (AVSpeechSynthesizer) to read your manual aloud. For the best experience, we recommend downloading the Daniel (Enhanced) voice.

How to get Daniel Enhanced

1️⃣ Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
2️⃣ Go to Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices
3️⃣ Tap English
4️⃣ Find Daniel and tap the download ⬇️ icon next to Enhanced
5️⃣ Once downloaded, POH Reader will use it automatically
🎧 Controls: Tap Read page to read the current page, Read section for the whole chapter, Stop to pause, and Resume to continue where you left off. Adjust speed with the slider.

You can also use the microphone button to ask questions by voice — hold the button, speak your question, then release. POH Reader uses native iOS speech recognition for accurate results.

Zoom & Pan

Navigate performance charts and diagrams

POH Reader supports full pinch-to-zoom with pan, perfect for reading performance charts and weight & balance diagrams.

🤏

Pinch to zoom

Place two fingers on the page and pinch in or out to zoom. The page zooms toward your fingers.

👆

Single finger pan

When zoomed in, drag with one finger to move around the page in any direction.

📄

Auto-reset on page change

Zoom resets automatically when you navigate to a new page.

💡 Tip: Use zoom on performance charts — pinch in on the takeoff distance graph and drag to find your exact intersection point.
